jquery.swappable.js

What does this do?

It turns an element into an input. Usually reserved for little paragraphs or editing fields and having a "onblur save". Pretty cool.

Why is that cool?

Well it makes it simple to add a "click-to-edit" feature. This is more of a case for fun UX than solving an actual problem.
